Faraway Fansite (Faraway Story)

About This Site

This is a site promoting the PC game, Faraway Story (悠遠物語), as well as a Capstone project for the Web Design for Everybody Coursera specialization. Using the Bootstrap framework, some Javascript and CSS, and a buttload of screenshots, this site will show off the characters of the game, demystify the game's mechanics, and try to be responsive to different screen sizes.

About Faraway Story

Faraway Story is a Wolf RPG Editor game made by Fantasy Factory and translated to English by vgperson, featuring a complex Action battle system, shopkeeping, Atelier-like crafting and task management, and hilariously unfitting use of boss music. The game is still a work in progress, with the first half and a few chapters of the second half currently playable.

The game follows the story of Pia, an humble pharmacist determined to save Port Fina from a mysterious disease. On the way to town, she notices the floating Faraway Continent, and is perplexed by how all the locals are all used to this phenomenon, as well as their conflicting stories about it. Little does she know that her coming-of-age story will get her involved with the continent, the continent's eccentric custodian, and all sorts of colorful characters from around the world.
